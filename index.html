The SCH Framework

Every report is grounded in the same three analytical lenses — developed by Source Code Holdings. Not a personality quiz. A structured read of how a person or organisation actually functions under pressure.

Lens 01

Observer Code

Maps the ratio of internal vs external awareness — how much a person processes inward versus how much they project outward. Determines communication style, conflict response, and blind spots.

Lens 02

Balance Scale

Identifies where energy and attention are overextended versus underinvested. Shows structural load imbalances in relationships, work, and self-management before they become crises.

Lens 03

Drift Analysis

Measures deviation from original values, positioning, or intent. Useful for businesses that have drifted from their founding clarity and individuals who've gradually shifted from core patterns.
