Foundation

How this practice is structured and what it's built on.

Source Code Holdings

Riclee Digital is a brand vertical under SCH — the parent entity that houses Umni Systems (trading software) and VG Codeworks (automotive). All work is independent and owner-operated.

Owner-Operated

Every project is handled directly by Ric Lee. There are no junior handoffs, no teams, and no inflated quotes to cover agency overhead. You work with the person doing the work.

The SCH structure exists to keep disciplines clean and separate. Riclee Digital handles consultancy, design, and development. Umni Systems handles trading tools and signals. VG Codeworks handles the automotive side. One person. Three verticals. All under one roof.

Practical By Default

No unnecessary complexity. Projects are scoped clearly, built to spec, and delivered. If something isn't needed, it won't be sold. The goal is functional output, not billable hours.
